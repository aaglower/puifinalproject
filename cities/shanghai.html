Welcome.

Our Shanghai-themed restaurant offers a contemporary take on classic Chinese cuisine, blending traditional ingredients and flavors with innovative cooking techniques and presentation.

Tony Lu

Chef Tony Lu is a renowned chef specializing in Shanghai cuisine. He is the owner and head chef of restaurants such as Fu 1088 and Yong Yi Ting, both located in Shanghai. Chef Lu is known for his innovative approach to traditional Shanghai dishes, often incorporating modern techniques and global ingredients. He has been awarded two Michelin stars for his restaurant, Yong Yi Ting.
