Welcome.

Our NYC-themed restaurant celebrates the diverse culinary traditions and flavors of the city that never sleeps, showcasing classic American dishes alongside global influences and modern twists.

Marcus Samuelsson

Chef Marcus Samuelsson is a highly acclaimed chef and restaurateur, known for his innovative approach to classic American cuisine. Born in Ethiopia and raised in Sweden, he has worked at some of the world's top restaurants, including Aquavit and Le Bernardin. He currently owns several restaurants in New York City, including Red Rooster Harlem and Streetbird Rotisserie.

Specials

Pastrami Reuben

A classic NYC sandwich featuring pastrami, Swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and Thousand Island dressing on rye bread, served with a side of potato chips.

Spicy Fried Chicken and Waffles

Buttermilk fried chicken tossed in a spicy sauce and served atop a fluffy waffle, drizzled with maple syrup and garnished with scallions.

Lobster Roll

Fresh lobster meat tossed with lemon mayo, celery, and herbs, served on a buttered and toasted split-top bun.
