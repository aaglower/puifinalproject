Welcome.

Our Mumbai-themed restaurant celebrates the vibrant and diverse flavors of Indian cuisine, specifically inspired by the bustling city of Mumbai, known for its street food and multicultural influences.

Vikas Khanna

Chef Vikas Khanna is an award-winning Indian chef, restaurateur, cookbook author, and humanitarian. He has received multiple Michelin stars for his New York City restaurant, Junoon, and has been recognized for his work with various charities. Khanna is known for his modern and innovative approach to Indian cuisine, while also honoring traditional techniques and flavors.

Specials

Pav Bhaji

A popular Mumbai street food dish consisting of spiced mashed vegetables (such as potatoes, tomatoes, and peas) served with soft, buttery bread rolls.

Chicken Tikka Masala

A classic Indian dish featuring marinated chicken in a creamy tomato-based sauce with aromatic spices, served with fluffy basmati rice.

Vada Pav

Another beloved Mumbai street food, this vegetarian sandwich consists of a deep-fried potato fritter (vada) served between two slices of bread (pav) with chutney and spices.
